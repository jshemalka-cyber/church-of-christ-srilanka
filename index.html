Our Core Beliefs

Biblical Authority

We believe the Bible is our sole guide for faith and practice, with "no creed but Christ."

A Cappella Worship

Our worship services feature only vocal music, as we seek to follow the New Testament example.

Weekly Communion

We observe the Lord's Supper every Sunday, in remembrance of Jesus\' sacrifice.
